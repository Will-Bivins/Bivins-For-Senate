The Official Will Bivins College of Business Senate Campaign

Dear Students, Teachers, and members of the University of Louisville Community. My name is Will Bivins and I am happy to present to you my campaign for College of Business Senator.

The reason that I chose to use a website to present my information is that I believe technology is a centerpiece for communicating ideas in a corporate setting. Over the past decades, we have seen an increase in new technological tools in the workplace, and I intend to bring those concepts to the College of Business Senate. While I am not claiming that I will simply build the College of Business a website for student outreach, this is meant to be an example as to how projects can be conducted.

This is an interactive, live website built from scratch with Notion to organize ideas and resources, and GitHub to execute the files. My campaign is an example project organized and executed from Notion.

Notion is a strong tool to convey ideas, organize information, project timelines, team resources, and more. It also introduces users to entry-level coding mechanics that help build logical thinking and organizational skills. Throughout this entry, I will demonstrate various mechanics available in Notion, while explaining who I am and why I feel qualified to represent you in Senate.

As a business student, I believe in using technology for presenting business-related technical information, company goals, and project management. Welcome to my campaign!
